Drought Prediction





Drought generally means an acute water shortage. It is an even of prolonged water supply shortage, whether due to below-average rainfall, surface water or ground water. These events are serious natural disaster that have a substantial impact on the environment, ecosystem, economy and agriculture of the affected region.

In order to manage and reduce the losses related to drought through drought prevention and disaster reduction measures, capability to predict drought accurately is imperative. While the problem and the trend has been studied very well in the literatures, it is unknown if drought can be predicted precisely with machine learning models with the help of the weather data. For the purpose of answering this question, a real-world public dataset has been used on this model and different levels of drought are predicted using 90 days of 6 meteorological indicators as the predictors.
